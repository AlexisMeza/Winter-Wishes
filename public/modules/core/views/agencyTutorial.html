Tracking Form Tutorial

This page will help you easily add winter wishes letters and submit the tracking form to the New York Cares Winter Wishes Team.

Your tracking form is due on {{dueDate | date:'fullDate' }}.

Before you begin, organize your letters by the number on the tracking label. This will make it easier for you to enter the data.

Adding a letter

Fill out the form according to the participant's letter, then press to save your progress and move on to the next letter. All fields are required. You will not be able to continue if a field is left blank or if the data you entered does not meet the requirements.

Editing a letter

Use
to quickly find the letter you want to edit. Then, click on the row of the letter you wish to edit. The form fields will populate with the details from the letter you selected. Make your changes, then press to proceed.

Submitting your Tracking Form

Click to notify the Winter Wishes Team that your tracking form is complete. You will not be able to make any further changes.

Once submitted, click to download a copy of your tracking form.
