Tracking Form Review

This page will help you easily review a tracking form once it has been submitted.

Once the tracking form and letters have been submitted: First, make sure the letters are organized by the number on the tracking label. This will make it easier for you to review the data. Then, click to begin reviewing.

Flagging a letter

Click to mark a sub par letter. Click if you change your mind.

Click if the majority of the letters are unacceptable. This will allow the agency contact to edit the tracking form again. In these cases, you may want to send the letters back to be rewritten.

Editing a letter

Use
to quickly find the letter you want to edit. Then, click on the row of the letter you wish to edit. The form fields will populate with the details from the letter you selected.
Correct any mistakes you caught, then press to update the entry and move on to the next letter.

Review Complete

Click to complete the review. You will not be able to make any further changes.

Once complete, click to download a copy of the tracking form.
